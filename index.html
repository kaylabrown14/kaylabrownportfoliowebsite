Concept

This is my display of posters through the eras for Graphic Design History. Below, you will see a variety of posters discussing different topics.

“The principles of true art is not to portray, but to evoke” – Jerzy Kosinski

All these posters vary from Wood Type, Arts and Crafts, Art Noveau, the Vienna Secession, and Modern Art.

Graphic Designer and Content Creator

Week 1: Wood Type Poster

For this poster, I took the concept from Made in America and brought it to Wood Type, using different typefaces.


Week 2: Arts and Crafts Poster

During the arts and crafts era, the posters designed were handmade, leading me to hand draw the font and design. This poster focuses on Consent.

Week 3: Art Noveau Poster

For this poster, I used my sister as a reference and designed the poster based around her book, Daydreaming, hence the word, Dream.

Week 4: Vienna Secession Poster

For my Vienna Seccession poster, I wanted to highlight the gender disparity between men and women by showing a hierarchy and difference in pattern.

Week 5: Modern Art Poster

The Modern Art Poster focuses on the news choosing the best stories to spotlight.
